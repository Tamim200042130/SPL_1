How To Play

Home Page Buttons

:-) Please play this game in "Full Screen Mode"(Press "F11") and "Zoom"('ctrl'+'+') it 100%.
:-) Press "PLAY_I" button to play "Simple Mode".
:-) Press "Play_II" button to play "Theory of Relativity Mode".
:-) Press "About Us" button to know about the developer of the game.
:-) You can share your experience with us through mail.

Spaceship Movement

:-) Press "Left Arrow" or "Num6" or "D" Key to move the spaceship "Left".
:-) Press "Right Arrow" or "Num4" or "E" Key to move the spaceship "Right".
:-) Press "Upper Arrow" or "Num8" or "W" or "Space" Key to throw "Bullets".

Rules for "PLAY_I/Simple Mode"

:-) After pressing "PLAY_I" button a "Alert Prompt" will come.You have to write your name there.
:-) Initially you have 10 "Bullet".When you fired a bullet the amount of bullet will decrease by 1.
:-) When the amount of bullet is 0 then you can not fired any bullet at that time.
:-) Collecting coins can increase the amount of bullets.1 coin = 1 bullet.
:-) Initially you have 3 "Life".If an "Alien Ship" pass the bottom line your life will decrease by 1.
:-) If "Life" become 0 then the will over and show your "Score"
:-) If you want to restart the game simply click "F5" key of the keyboard.
:-) Always try to beat your "Highest Score.Best of luck.


Rules for "PLAY_II/Theory of Relativity Mode"

:-) After pressing "PLAY_II" button a "Alert Prompt" will come.You have to write your name there.
:-) You can not destroy the star until you have the power to destroy it.
:-) If you go right then the power of the bullet will decrease.
:-) Collecting coins can increase the power of the bullets.
:-) Collect the coins as you can and destroy all the stars.
:-) There is no ending point of the game.
:-) If you want to restart the game simply click "F5" key of the keyboard.
:-) If you press "EndGame" button the game will be ended and show your score.
:-) Always try to destroy all the star after pressing "EndGame" button.Best of luck.
